About Acro

Acro is a discipline that combines gymnastics, dance, and acrobatics. It involves two or more people working together to create intricate and dynamic routines that showcase strength, balance, and flexibility. Acro is a fun and challenging sport that requires dedication, discipline, and teamwork.

If you're interested in learning acro, there are many resources available to help you get started. From local clubs and gyms to online tutorials and courses, there are many ways to start your acro journey.
